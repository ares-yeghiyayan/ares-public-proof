# Redaction Checklist

Before anything moves public, confirm it does NOT reveal:

- product architecture
- module names
- target customer strategy
- market positioning strategy
- decision workflow mechanics
- risk engine mechanics
- internal proof chain structure
- private prompts
- proprietary edge logic
- strategy allocation logic
- broker or execution details
- customer assumptions
- investor-private material
- screenshots of private repository state
- performance claims
- financial advice claims
- production readiness claims
