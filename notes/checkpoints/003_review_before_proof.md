# Review Before Proof Checkpoint

This checkpoint records a simple discipline rule:

Outputs should be reviewed before they are treated as proof.

## Why this matters

Visible work is not enough by itself. Useful proof needs small work units, clean boundaries, review notes, and a repeatable way to separate evidence from noise.

## What this checkpoint shows

- Work remains small and reviewable.
- Notes stay inside a safe boundary.
- Review discipline is part of the work.
- Public proof focuses on habits, not private details.

## Next focus

Keep the public surface clean, narrow, reviewable, and useful for understanding the operating rhythm.
